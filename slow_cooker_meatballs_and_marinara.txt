Chop first: 2 onions, minced 8 garlic cloves, minced 2 Tbsp minced fresh
oregano or 2 tsp dried 1/4 C minced fresh parsley




2 Tbsp olive oil 1/4 C tomato paste 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes 1/2 C dry
red wine &nbsp; 2 28 oz cans crushed tomatoes 1/2 C water (or better:
Vodka!)  2 Tbsp soy sauce 2 slices sandwich bread, torn into quarters
1/3 C whole milk 1 1/4 lbs 85% lean ground beef 4 oz. Italian sausage,
removed from casing 1/2 C grated parmesan cheese (1 oz), plus extra for
serving 2 large egg yolks Salt and Pepper 2 Tbsp chopped fresh basil 1
tsp sugar, plus extra as needed




Heat oil in 12-innch skillet over med-high heat. Add onions, tomato
paste, garlic, oregano, and red pepper flakes, and cook until onions
are softened and lightly browned, 8 - 10 minutes.

Transfer half of onion mixture to mixing bowl, set aside. Deglaze pan
with wine, and transfer to slow cooker. Add tomatoes, water/vodka,
and soy sauce to the slow cooker.

Add bread and milk to onion mixture and mash with a fork. Mix in ground
beef, sausage, parmesan, parsley, egg yolks, 3/4 tsp salt, and 1/2 tsp
pepper using hands. Roll into 1 1/2” meatballs (about 18 meatballs
total)

Microwave meatballs on plate on high for 5-7 minutes, until firm and
fat renders. Discard fat, place meatballs in slow cooker. Cover and cook
until meatballs are tender, 4 - 6 hours on low.

Let meatballs and sauce settle for five minutes. Skim fat from surface
using a large spoon. Stir in basil and sugar. Add salt, pepper and sugar
to taste if needed, and serve.
