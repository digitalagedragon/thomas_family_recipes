1 C heavy whipping cream
1/2 C whole milk
1 1/2 C canned pumpkin
1 C packed brown sugar
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves

Mix it all up and put it in the ice cream maker
