Mix the water, cornstarch, sugar and paprika in a bowl and stir to
combine.


Put a little oil in the skillet, and sautee the onion until soft and
translucent, about 8 minutes. Add garlic and stir until fragrant, about

Add water mixture and milk. Bring to a boil.
