1 English cucumber (340 g), unpeeled, very thinly sliced
1/8 cup (5g) finely chopped fresh dill
1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
1 tsp coarse kosher salt
1 tablespoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Toss it all together and refrigerate at least fifteen minutes
