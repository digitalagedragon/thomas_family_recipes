
Too much butter and a little too much liquid. Try 2/3 C buttermilk, 3/4 C butter, and 1/2 tsp salt


---




2 cups raspberries
1 1/4 C all-purpose flour (205 g)
1 C sugar
1 C buttermilk
1 C butter
2 eggs
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt


Preheat oven to 350 degrees


Melt butter (putting it in the preheating oven is a great way to do this)


Mix together raspberries and sugar. Let rest.


Put dry ingredients in a bowl and whisk together.


Crack eggs in a new bowl and whisk. Add melted butter slowly while whisking. Add buttermilk.


Add liquids to dry, and place the batter in the pan. Add raspberry mixture to pan, and back for 45 minutes or until cooked through in center.
