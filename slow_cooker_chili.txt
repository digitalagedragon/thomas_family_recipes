1 pound beef chuck, cut into 3/4-inch chunks 1 can (15 ounces) tomato
puree 1 cup dried black beans, rinsed 1 medium red onion, chopped 2
garlic cloves, minced 3 tablespoons chili powder Coarse salt and ground
pepper 1/4 cup sour cream, for serving



DIRECTIONS

In a 5- to 6-quart slow cooker, combine beef, tomato puree, beans, onion
(reserve 1 tablespoon for garnish), garlic, chili powder, 2 cups water,
2 teaspoons salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Cover, and cook on high,
6 hours (or on low, 8 hours). Serve chili topped with sour cream and
reserved onion.
