
One sandwich is 75g of tuna salad. This recipe yields eight sandwiches.


1 lbs well-done tuna, flaked 2 stalks celery, diced 120 g homemade
mayonnaise 3 T Dijon mustard 2 tsp finely chopped dill 1/4 medium onion,
finely chopped 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper 1/2 tsp salt


Mix it all together in a bowl.
