Ingredients

48 HERSHEY'S KISSES Brand Milk Chocolates
1/2 C shortening
3/4 C Creamy Peanut Butter
1/3 C granulated sugar
1/3 C light brown sugar
1 egg
2 Tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 C all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
Additional granulated sugar

Directions

1 Heat oven to 375Â°F. Remove wrappers from chocolates.

2 Beat shortening and peanut butter in large bowl until well blended. Add
1/3 cup granulated sugar and brown sugar; beat until fluffy. Add egg,
milk and vanilla; beat well. Stir together flour, baking soda and salt;
gradually beat into peanut butter mixture.

3 Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Roll in granulated sugar; place on
ungreased cookie sheet.

4 Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned. Immediately press a
chocolate into center of each
