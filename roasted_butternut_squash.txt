1 butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into 1” cubes Olive oil
8 cloves of garlic, chopped


Preheat oven to 400F


Toss the squash, olive oil, and garlic. Spread out on a baking sheet
with a piece of parchment paper on it. Salt and pepper. Place in over
and cook until tender and browned, 25 - 30 minutes.
