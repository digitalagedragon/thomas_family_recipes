Key Lime Cake:
  1 C Neutral Oil
  3 large Eggs
  1 1/3 C Whole Milk
  2 Tbsp Fresh Lime Zest
  1 Tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
  2 C (396g) Sugar
  2 C (240g) Flour
  1 tsp Baking Powder
  1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  1 tsp Salt

Lime Cream Cheese Frosting:
  1/2 C (1 stick) unsalted butter (softened)
  8 oz Cream Cheese (softened)
  4 - 4 1/2 cups Powdered Sugar
  1 Tablespoon Fresh Lime Juice
  1/2 teaspoon Fresh Lime Zest
  Fresh Limes (sliced for garnish if you can be arsed)

Preheat oven to 325 degrees.

In a large mixing bowl, add oil, eggs, whole milk, lime zest, and lime
juice. Cream for 2 minutes.

In a large bowl, add sugar, flour, baking powder, baking soda, and
salt. Stir into egg mixture and fold.

Spray one 9x13", two 9", or three 8" cake pans with nonstick cooking
spray. Place batter into baking pans. Cooking times:

  1 9x13":  X
  2    9":  30-36 minutes
  3    8":  20-26 minutes

Turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely before applying
frosting.


To make Lime Cream Cheese Frosting:
In a large bowl, cream together butter and cream cheese for 4-5
minutes until light and fluffy, scraping sides of bowl often. Add
powdered sugar, lime juice, lime zest, and cream together. Chill.

If baked in 1 9x13" pan, cut cooled cake in half to make two layers.

Spread frosting on top of one cooled cake layer. Place the other
cooled cake layer on top and spread frosting all over. Garnish with
fresh lime slices.
