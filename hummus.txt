3 cloves garlic
1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, 1/2 C of the liquid reserved
Juice of one lemon
3 tablespoons tahini

1/4 teaspoon salt

black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons olive oil


Chop the garlic in the food processor. Â Then, add everything (including the reserved liquid) except the olive oil and blend it until it's hummus.


Transfer to a bowl, and then add the olive oil on top, and stir it in.
