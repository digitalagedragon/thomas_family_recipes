
1/2 onion, minced  4 cloves garlic, smashed  3/4 cup bourbon whiskey


 2 cups ketchup  1/4 cup tomato paste  1 Tbsp cider vinegar  1/4
cup Worcestershire sauce

1/4 tsp tabasco sauce


Place onion, garlic and whiskey in a saucepan. Simmer for ten
minutes. Puree with stick blender. Then, add other ingredients and simmer
for 20. Cool and bottle.
