
1 pkg (18.5 oz) yellow cake mix 1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted 1
egg 2/3 cup reserved cake mix 1/2 cup brown sugar 1 1/2 cup dark Karo
syrup 1 tsp vanilla 3 eggs 1 cup coursely cut pecans


Grease 13 x 9 in. cake pan.  Rserver 2/3 cup cake mix for filling. Mix
first 3 ingredients. Press into prepared pan.  Bake at 350 degrees F
for 15-20 minutes until light brown.


Meanwhile prepare filling from next 5 ingredients. Pour over partialy
baked crust und sprinkle with pecans. Return to oven for 30 - 35 minutes
until well set. Cool. Cut into bars or squares.


Makes about 4 dozen.
