
3 onions, chopped 1/4 C tomato paste 6 garlic cloves, chopped 1 tsp
dried thyme 1/3 C all-purpose flour 3 C braising liquid (see below) 1/3
C soy sauce 2 bay leaves 4 lbs beef stew meat 1 lbs carrots, peeled, and
sliced 1” thick 12 small potatoes 1/2 tsp salt (may need more) Pepper


Heat oil in skillet. Add onions, garlic, and tomato paste. Sauté
until onions are brown, 8-10 minutes. Add flour, and cook for one
minute. Deglaze with 1/2 the braising liquid, whisking to remove any
flour lumps.


Dump everything in the slow cooker and cook for 9-11 hours on low or
5-7 hours on high.


Let settle for five minutes and skim off any fat; serve.


Braising liquid suggestions:

1 1/2 C chicken broth 1 1/2 C beef broth
