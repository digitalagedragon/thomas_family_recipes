Canola oil, or bacon fat
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
1 tsp salt
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
3 cups (3 cans) drained cooked pinto beans, cooking liquid reserved

Sweat the onion 8-10 minutes until translucent. Add everything and cook
for 3-5 minutes (until warm). Finally, mash with a potato masher or
blend with immersion blender.
