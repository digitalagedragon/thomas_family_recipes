Ingredients


6 tablespoons butter 1 onion, thinly sliced 12 ounces button mushrooms,
halved 4 cups chicken stock 1 sprig parsley 2 ounces sherry salt and
pepper

Procedures


1


Over medium heat, melt two tablespoons of the butter in a saucepan. Toss
in the onion and cook until soft but not browned.


2


Toss in the remaining butter and then add the mushrooms. Cook for
8 minutes.


3


Pour in the chicken stock, add the parsley, and bring to a boil. When
bubbling, reduce to a simmer and cook for an hour.


4


Pour soup into a blender (you might need to do this in stages), and
process until smooth. Return to the saucepan and bring to a simmer. Pour
in the sherry, and season with salt and pepper.Â
