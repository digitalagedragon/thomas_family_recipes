
Gambas al Ajillo


1 C olive oil
6 cloves garlic, chopped
2 bay leaves
2 Tbsp red pepper flakes
2 Tbsp minced fresh parsley
1 1/2 lbs large shrimp (31 to 40 per pound), peeled and deveined
Salt and Pepper


Place an oven rack in the middle position and heat oven to 500 degrees.


Pour the oil into a shallow casserole dish (roughly 11" x 7”). Add the garlic, bay leaves, pepper flakes, and parsley, ad bake until the garlic is fragrant and sizzling, 2 - 4 minutes.


Add the shrimp to the sizzling oil and continue to back, stirring once, until the shrimp are just cooked through, 2 - 3 minutes. Remove and discard the bay leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve immediately from the baking dish.
