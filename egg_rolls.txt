1 pound ground pork
1 inch fresh ginger grated
1 clove garlic, pressed
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon Chinese 5 spice (optional)
1 tablespoon soy sauce
2 1/2 cups packaged fresh coleslaw (or 2 1/2 cups finely chopped
  cabbage & shredded carrots)

12 egg roll wrappers (6 inch square)
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons water
1 quart canola oil for frying

Over medium heat, cook pork, ginger, garlic, onion powder, chinese 5
spice (if using) and soy sauce until no pink remains. Set aside.
